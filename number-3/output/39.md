You raise your hands slowly, palms open. Beside you, your uncle does the same, though you can see his jaw tighten with frustration.

"Good choice," one of the men says. "Now step outside. Slowly."

You move toward the entrance, rain immediately soaking through your clothes. The men keep their rifles aimed at your chests as they march you away from the shelter, deeper into the storm.

"What about the others?" you whisper to your uncle.

"Quiet," the second man snaps.

You glance back. Thea and Grumsh are still inside, presumably asleep. The TIME CORP agents do not seem interested in them. Only in you.

The trek through the jungle is brutal. Mud sucks at your shoes. Branches whip your face. Lightning flashes overhead, illuminating a path that climbs steadily upward toward the volcano.

Then you hear it. A low hum, growing louder with each step. Electrical. Powerful.

The trees part, and you stop in your tracks.

Before you rises a massive metal structure built into the volcano's slope. Tesla coils tower against the sky, crackling with energy. Cables snake across the ground like mechanical roots. Armed guards patrol walkways and platforms, their black uniforms identical to your captors'.

A hidden base. Right here on the island.

"Move," the man behind you orders.
