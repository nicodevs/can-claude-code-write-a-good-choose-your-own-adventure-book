You twist in the air, channeling every ounce of magic you have left into a single desperate spell.

"PROTEGO MAXIMA!"

The shield blooms around you—a sphere of shimmering blue light—just as the courtyard rushes up to meet you. You hit the cobblestones with a bone-jarring CRUNCH. The shield absorbs most of the impact, shattering into a thousand glittering fragments that dissolve like morning frost.

But not all of it.

Pain explodes through your body. Your ribs. Your shoulder. Your left leg twists beneath you at an angle that makes your stomach lurch. You try to scream, but the air has been driven from your lungs. The world tilts and spins. Rain pounds your face.

Voices. Distant at first, then closer.

"Someone fell!"

"Is that—is that a student?"

"Get the healers! NOW!"

Faces swim above you, blurred and frightened. You recognize some of them. Fellow third-years. A prefect. Someone is pressing a hand to your forehead. Someone else is shouting orders.

You try to speak. Try to say Aldric's name. But darkness creeps in at the edges of your vision, soft and warm and insistent. Your last thought before unconsciousness takes you is of those burning coal eyes disappearing into the storm.
